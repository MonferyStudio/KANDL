<h1>KANDL — A Roguelike Trading Game</h1>

<p><strong>Trade. Profit. Prestige. Repeat.</strong></p>

<p>You've just been hired at a ruthless trading firm. Hit the profit quota before time runs out — or get fired. Every run is a new year, every day a new opportunity. How long can you survive?</p>

<hr>

<h2>Features</h2>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Roguelike runs</strong> — beat the quota to keep going with harder targets, fail and spend prestige points to come back stronger</li>
  <li><strong>20+ companies across 5 sectors</strong> with realistic price movements and breaking news events</li>
  <li><strong>Market signals</strong> (RSI, support/resistance, momentum) to guide your trades — but watch out for Signal Jammer events</li>
  <li><strong>Daily upgrade picks</strong> with 5 rarity tiers, a permanent shop, and sector-specific bonuses</li>
  <li><strong>FinTok social feed</strong> — AI influencers post stock tips, some are geniuses, some are clowns</li>
  <li><strong>Prestige system</strong> — earn points each day and from achievements, unlock permanent bonuses across runs</li>
  <li><strong>Auto-save</strong>, manual export, responsive design, English &amp; French</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Tips</h2>

<ol>
  <li><strong>Watch the signals</strong> — green badges mean buy opportunities</li>
  <li><strong>Diversify</strong> — don't put all your cash in one stock</li>
  <li><strong>Read the news</strong> — events affect entire sectors</li>
  <li><strong>Pick upgrades wisely</strong> — sector shields protect against crashes</li>
</ol>

<p><em>A game about reading charts, trusting your gut, and pretending you know what you're doing.</em></p>
