v0.2 - The Prestige Update

I haven't slept much lately, but it was worth it. The old prestige shop is gone. In its place: a full talent tree with 324 nodes across 5 branches, new trading mechanics, new daily upgrades, and a much deeper progression system.

The Talent Tree

When you fail a quota, instead of a flat grid of upgrades, you now get an interactive talent tree you can pan and zoom around. Two interconnected trees side by side:

  • General Tree (left) - Your core trading skills, split into branches
  • Sector Tree (right) - 16 sector specializations arranged in a circle

Each node costs Prestige Points. You earn PP by surviving days. Buy nodes from the center outward — each one requires its parent to be unlocked first. All effects kick in at the start of each new run.

The Branches

Trader (green) - The biggest branch, packed with new mechanics:

  • Extra position slots, all the way up to unlimited positions at the capstone
  • Stop Loss & Take Profit - Set automatic sell triggers. Fine-tune thresholds, add trailing stops, partial profit-taking, and a safety net that softens your first stop loss each run
  • Limit Orders - Place buy/sell orders in advance at a target price. Attach SL/TP to them for fully automated trades
  • Leverage - Multiply your gains (and losses). Start at 1.25x, unlock up to 3x. A margin shield gives you extra breathing room before liquidation
  • Tempo Trading - Bonuses for your trading rhythm. Quick flips reward same-day sells, patience bonuses reward holding 3-7 days, and the Scalper perk removes fees on day trades
  • Compound Interest - Your idle cash earns daily interest. Stack it up to 0.5% per day
  • Winning Streak - Consecutive profitable trades build a stacking bonus. Start at +5% per win (max 3 stacks), upgrade to +8% (max 6), and unlock Resilient to only halve your streak on a loss instead of losing it all
  • Profit Multipliers - Straight-up percentage boosts to all your winning trades

Survival (blue) - The branch for players who refuse to die:

  • Extra Days - More time to meet each quota, up to +3 extra days
  • Extra Lives - Survive a failed quota and keep going. Stack up to 3 lives
  • Quota Relief - Lower your quota targets by up to 10%
  • Skip Boost - Get more rewards when you skip a quota, with stacking bonuses for consecutive skips
  • Loss Recovery - Recover a percentage of your losses automatically (up to 10%)
  • Overtime - Emergency extra days when you fail a quota, plus a Second Wind buff after using a life
  • PP Boost - Earn up to 35% more Prestige Points each run
  • Early Bird - Bonus PP for finishing quotas ahead of schedule, with a Speedrunner capstone that doubles PP if every quota was early
  • Momentum - Consecutive quota successes give a stacking profit bonus, and the capstone lets your streak persist through lives
  • Last Stand - The ultimate capstone: an extra life and -25% quota when you resurrect

Automation (purple) - Unlock up to 10 robot trading slots along the main spine, with global upgrades branching off:

  • Bulk Discount - Reduce robot upgrade costs for all robots (up to -30%)
  • Boot Camp - All robots start pre-leveled (up to Lv.3)
  • Auto-Collect - Automatically collect all robot wallets
  • Startup Fund - Robots start with extra cash (up to +$200)
  • Calibration - Global win rate boost for all robots (up to +8%)
  • Overclock - Robots trade faster (up to +50% speed)

Intelligence (pink) - Knowledge is profit. This branch splits into 4 sub-branches:

  • Shop Mastery (spine) - Free rerolls, rarity boosts, extra upgrade choices, and the capstone grants a guaranteed Rare+ upgrade at the start of each run
  • Secret Informant - Free daily tips, cheaper intel, precision boosts, and the ability to see exact tip accuracy percentages
  • FintTok - Boost influencer accuracy, unlock extra influencer slots, and flag unreliable tips with a warning badge
  • News & Events - Extra daily news, block one negative event per run, and a Disinfo Shield that makes you immune to market manipulation and signal jammers

Sectors (orange) - 16 sector spokes in a full circle, each with 3 tiers of bonuses:

  • Profit boosts, loss shields, and passive income per sector
  • Capstone nodes at the tip of each spoke that double all bonuses for that sector

New Mechanics

These features are unlocked through the Trader branch of the talent tree:

Stop Loss / Take Profit - Automatically sell positions when they hit a loss or profit threshold. No more watching helplessly as a good trade turns bad. You choose the percentages.

Limit Orders - Place a pending buy or sell order at a specific price. The cash is reserved, and when the market hits your target, the trade executes automatically. You can even pre-attach SL/TP to the order.

Leverage - Trade with borrowed money. A 2x leverage means your $1000 acts like $2000 — double the gains, but double the losses too. If your position drops too far, it gets liquidated automatically. Use with caution!

New Daily Upgrades

Three new upgrade families focused on sector specialization, giving you more strategic depth each run:

Sector Insight (5 rarities, sector-specific) - Bias your daily news toward a specific sector. At common rarity you get a 20% chance to redirect news to your sector. At legendary, you get guaranteed sector news every morning plus a preview of what's coming.

Extra News (3 static upgrades) - More information means better decisions:

  • Morning Edition (uncommon) - +1 news at market open
  • Evening Edition (rare) - +1 mid-day news
  • 24h News Cycle (epic) - +1 news at both morning and mid-day

Sector Dominance (5 rarities, sector-specific) - Rewards concentrating your portfolio. Each position in a sector adds a profit bonus (2-15% per position depending on rarity). The legendary version also cuts fees when buying in that sector.

Winning Streak (5 rarities, global) - Boost your consecutive win bonus. Higher rarities replace lower ones automatically. At common you get +2% per win, scaling up to +25% per consecutive profitable trade at legendary. Stacks with the Winning Streak talent branch for massive combo potential.

Chart Improvements

  • SL/TP lines on chart — When you have stop loss or take profit active, dashed horizontal lines show their trigger levels directly on the stock chart (red for SL, green for TP), with small labels showing the percentage
  • Zoom & Pan — Pinch to zoom in/out on the candlestick chart, swipe horizontally to scroll through history, and double-tap to reset. Desktop users can use the scroll wheel to zoom. See as few as 10 candles or as many as the full history
  • Auto Orders in Order Panel — The SL/TP controls are now integrated directly into the order panel as a collapsible "Auto Orders" section, right above the buy/sell button. No more hunting for them in a separate section

UI Improvements

  • New visual positions bar in the portfolio summary — see your open/available slots at a glance with color-coded indicators (green for long, red for short)
  • Configurable notification threshold — New slider in Settings to control price alert sensitivity (5% to 50%). Default raised from 5% to 10% to reduce notification spam in late game
  • Talent tree has a beautiful background with grid pattern and ambient glow effects
  • Branch labels appear progressively as you unlock nodes
  • Sector spoke labels show once you unlock the sector hub
  • Double-tap a node to buy it instantly, single-tap for details
  • FintTok now flags unreliable influencers with a warning badge (requires Intelligence talent)
  • Secret Informant shows exact accuracy % and free tip badges (requires Intelligence talents)
  • Daily upgrade carousel on mobile for easier browsing

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed 4 sectors (Tech, Consumer, Real Estate, Telecom) not showing unlocked companies despite having the tiers purchased — sector IDs in the talent tree now correctly map to the company database
  • Fixed "Extra News" talents (from Intelligence branch) only boosting morning news — they now also boost mid-day news as intended
  • Removed redundant "Full Combo" talent node that duplicated Smart Orders functionality
  • Fixed daily upgrade replacement logic for non-sector templates — global upgrades like Winning Streak now correctly replace lower-rarity versions
  • Fixed "Trade of the Day" showing up even when you didn't make any trades
  • Fixed shop upgrade values getting stuck at $999999 instead of showing the real price
  • Fixed prestige shop camera centering — the view now properly centers between both trees on open
  • Fixed position count display — now correctly reflects bonus slots from the talent tree
  • Fixed save/load for the new prestige system — old saves are automatically converted with full PP refund
  • Old saves with renamed sector talent nodes are automatically migrated (no PP lost)
  • ...and plenty of other individual bugs I forgot about ^^

Save Compatibility

If you had an existing save with the old prestige upgrades: all your spent PP have been refunded. You'll start with a fresh tree and all your points ready to spend however you like. No progress lost.
